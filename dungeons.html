Features

Dungeons

Dungeons are extremely ancient and dangerous areas spread around the map. Each contains a unique theme and requires tedious twists, perilous jumps and defeating a boss to conquer the dungeon, earning bookoo's of XP and loot.

Fighting

Each dungeon contains tons of extremely strong mobs that must be defeated to proceed. Some have special abilities, others have brute strength.

Puzzle and Parkour

Dungeons also have special sections that require brainpower or parkour to continue. Solve anything from simple to complex puzzles and problems to keep going through the dungeon.

Rewards

Once you've gotten past the fighting, puzzle and parkour section of the dungeon and defeated the boss, you'll earn tons of emeralds and experience points to help you level up.
