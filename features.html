Features





Custom Dungeons

Dungeons are extremely ancient and dangerous areas spread around the map. Each contains a unique theme and requires tedious twists, perilous jumps and defeating a boss to conquer the dungeon, earning bookoo's of XP and loot.

Custom Towns

Once King Gabriel was kidnapped, the land of Danne fell to ruin. Civilization seemed to crumble right along with the walls of the many glorious cities in Danne. Under Prince Traeton's leadership, the towns began to regroup and rebuild- each focusing on a particular area to the future success of Danne. Visit one of the many towns in Danne to experience each ones unique atmosphere, culture and way of life.

King Gaebriel's Castle

Residence to His Heinousness Prince Traeton, King Gaebriel's Castle greets newcomers to Danne with its massive vault of weapons and defense. Villagers enjoy complete and total protection through the thick walls and high security.

Du'berville

With the central focus on Agriculture, Du'berville contributes to the success of Danne by harvesting crops and caring and raising livestock.
